Great Lakes MRI of Michigan is Here For You

Serving the community since 2002, Great Lakes MRI of Michigan is the only free-standing, independent diagnostic imaging center specializing in MRI, MRA scans and Arthrogram procedures in Warren.

Our Mission

Great Lakes MRI of Michigan is dedicated to providing high-quality, timely and compassionate care that enhances the knowledge of disease and health through MRI and MRA imaging.

Our Vision

To become the leading radiological expert in Michigan by creating an environment conducive to the personal and professional development of our physicians and staff and by acknowledging and fulfilling the unique needs of our patients and referring providers.

Our Values

Compassion: We respect the unique and individual needs of our patients and referring providers.
Customer Service: Patients are our top priority.
Courtesy: We provide a caring & respectful environment.
Character: We are compassionate and honest.
Creativity: We encourage innovation and open communication.

Radiologists

Our board certified radiologists specialize in neuro, orthopedic and musculoskeletal radiology, which are interpreted by the Cleveland Clinic Department of eRadiology and local radiologists.

Our MRI unit is a state-of-the-art Philips 1.5T short bore which minimizes patient discomfort and claustrophobia.

We equip our facility with the most modern and comfortable machine and are constantly updating to keep pace with the leaps in imaging technology.

We have a staff of board certified radiologists who pledge their results to the referring physician with accuracy and next day turnaround of reports.

STAT referrals are prioritized with verbal results and expedited reporting.
